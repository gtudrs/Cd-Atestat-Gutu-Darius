Focaccia

Ingredients:

  • 500 g all-purpose flour
  • 325 ml warm water
  • 10 g salt
  • 7 g active dry yeast
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil
  • 1 tsp (5 g) sugar
  • 1 tsp (5 g) dried rosemary (optional)
  • Coarse sea salt for garnish

Steps:

  • Step 1: In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until frothy.
  • Step 2: In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil, then mix until a sticky dough forms.
  • Step 3: Knead the dough for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  • Step 4: Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for about 1 hour until doubled in size.
  • Step 5: Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F). Transfer the dough to a greased baking sheet and press it gently to form an even layer.
  • Step 6: Dimple the surface with your fingertips, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with rosemary and coarse salt.
  • Step 7: Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown. Let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.
