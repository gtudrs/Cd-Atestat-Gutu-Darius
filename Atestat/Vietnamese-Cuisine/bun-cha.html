Bun Cha

Ingredients:

  • 400 g ground pork
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp (15 g) sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp (5 g) black pepper
  • 1 tsp (5 g) lemongrass, finely chopped
  • 200 g rice vermicelli noodles
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • Fresh herbs (mint, cilantro, Thai basil)
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) nuoc cham dipping sauce

Steps:

  • Step 1: In a bowl, mix ground pork with fish sauce, soy sauce, sugar, garlic, black pepper, and lemongrass. Let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  • Step 2: Shape the marinated pork into small patties and grill them over medium heat until golden brown and cooked through, about 5 minutes per side.
  • Step 3: Meanwhile, cook the rice vermicelli noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse under cold water.
  • Step 4: Arrange the noodles in serving bowls along with cucumber slices and fresh herbs.
  • Step 5: Place the grilled pork patties on top of the noodles.
  • Step 6: Serve with nuoc cham dipping sauce on the side, allowing each person to pour as desired.
  • Step 7: Enjoy this flavorful Vietnamese dish with a balance of sweet, salty, and tangy flavors!
