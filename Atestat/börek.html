Börek

Ingredients:

  • 6 sheets of phyllo dough
  • 200 g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup (60 ml) olive oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup (60 ml) chopped parsley
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) salt
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) black pepper

Steps:

  • Step 1: Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Lightly grease a baking sheet.
  • Step 2: In a bowl, mix the crumbled feta cheese, chopped parsley, salt, and black pepper.
  • Step 3: In another bowl, whisk together the yogurt, olive oil, and egg to make a sauce.
  • Step 4: Lay one sheet of phyllo dough on a flat surface and brush with the yogurt mixture. Repeat with three more layers.
  • Step 5: Evenly distribute the cheese filling across the dough, then roll it tightly into a log and place on the baking sheet.
  • Step 6: Brush the top with more yogurt mixture for a golden crust.
  • Step 7: Bake for 30-35 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  • Step 8: Let it cool slightly before slicing and serving warm.
