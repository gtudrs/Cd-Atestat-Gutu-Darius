Fasole cu Cârnați

Ingredients:

  • 500 g dried white beans, soaked overnight
  • 300 g smoked sausages, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400 g) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp (5 g) paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) vegetable oil
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps:

  • Step 1: Drain and rinse the soaked beans. Place them in a large pot with fresh water and bring to a boil. Simmer for about 45 minutes until tender, then drain.
  • Step 2: In a separate pan, heat the vegetable oil and sauté the chopped onion, grated carrot, and minced garlic until soft and fragrant.
  • Step 3: Add the sliced smoked sausages and cook for about 5 minutes until slightly browned.
  • Step 4: Stir in the diced tomatoes, paprika, bay leaf, and cooked beans. Mix well and let everything simmer together for 20-25 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  • Step 5: Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  • Step 6: Remove the bay leaf before serving and garnish with freshly chopped parsley.
  • Step 7: Serve hot with crusty bread or polenta for a hearty Romanian meal.
